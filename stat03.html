Statistic Number Three!

All you never wanted to know about the World of Oranges.

Tons of oranges that have been exported in these states from 2018-2019 in relation to 2017-2018

How many tons of oranges have been exported in these states from 2018-2019 in relation to 2017-2018?

As we can see, california has a very average export of oranges, staying strong at 1.880 tons this year, which is 112 tons less than in the year 2017.

Texas is very weak in terms of orange export with only 115 tons. They did although increase their rate by 9 tons since 2017. Congrats Texas.

Florida is our personal favourite because of their extreme passion for oranges. With 3.330 tons and an increase of 101 tons since last year, Florida has earned it's top spot as the king of oranges in the U.S.

All in all, the U.S has exported 5.325 tons of oranges last year, sadly thats 2 tons less than in 2017.
